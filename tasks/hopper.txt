Make the one-legged hopper hop to the right (positive x) as fast as possible while staying upright and "healthy", with smooth, low-energy actions.

Observation layout (11 dims):
- obs[0]: z-position (height) of the torso
- obs[1]: angle of the torso
- obs[2]: angle of the thigh joint
- obs[3]: angle of the leg joint
- obs[4]: angle of the foot joint
- obs[5]: forward (x-axis) velocity of the torso — this is ALREADY a velocity
- obs[6]: vertical (z-axis) velocity of the torso
- obs[7]: angular velocity of the torso angle
- obs[8]: angular velocity of the thigh joint
- obs[9]: angular velocity of the leg joint
- obs[10]: angular velocity of the foot joint

Action: 3 joint torques (thigh, leg, foot) in [-1, 1].

Task notes:
- To reward forward speed use next_obs[5] directly; it is a velocity, so do NOT
  difference it across steps.
- The episode terminates early if the hopper becomes unhealthy (torso height too
  low or body angle too extreme); max horizon is 1000 steps. Add a survival/alive
  bonus and reward keeping obs[0] (height) and obs[1] (torso angle) in a healthy
  upright range, so the policy does not earn a one-off forward burst and then fall.
- Penalise large control torques (energy).
