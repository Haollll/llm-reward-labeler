Make the HalfCheetah robot run to the right as fast as possible, with smooth actions, low energy use, and stable body posture.

Observation layout (17 dims):
- obs[0]: z-position (height) of the body
- obs[1]: angle of the body
- obs[2:8]: joint angles
- obs[8]: forward (x-axis) velocity of the body — this is ALREADY a velocity, not a position
- obs[9]: vertical (z-axis) velocity
- obs[10:17]: joint angular velocities

Action: 6 joint torques in [-1, 1].

Key point: to reward forward speed, use next_obs[8] directly. Do NOT compute a
position difference like (next_obs[8] - obs[8]); obs[8] is already the velocity,
so differencing it gives acceleration, which is wrong.
